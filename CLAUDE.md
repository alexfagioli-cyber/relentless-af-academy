- Dark mode only — warm charcoal + gradient (background #1A1A2E with diagonal gradient, surface #25253D with #363654 border, text #E8F0FE/#8BA3C4, champagne gold accent #E8C872)
- All secrets in .env.local, never hardcoded
- Server-side scoring only for assessments
